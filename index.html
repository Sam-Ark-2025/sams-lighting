What I do
Services

  • Custom design & color planning (warm white, color, or combos)
  • Roof lines, ridges & peaks, gutters, dormers, porches
  • Trees, bushes, yard features, and storefronts
  • Timers & smart plugs — you can set them, or I can set them for you
  • Safe ladder & roof work, cleanup, and takedown
  • Optional labeled storage for easy re-install next year

I’m up for whatever light hanging you want, but most commonly I outline roof lines of houses or buildings. Every install uses pro-grade LED strands and clips sized to your roof line for a clean, repeatable layout — no dangling cords, no hot spots.

Serving Milwaukee, WI and nearby communities. Installation times are arranged during the process at a time convenient for both of us.
